Overview

I live in Taiwan, because I believe it's the best place to live on Earth for most people. Things I like to do here include going to g0v hackathons, taking pictures, riding motorcycles in the mountains, recording ambient sounds, contributing to open source streetviews with Mapillary, riding ubikes on the riverside, coworking at cafes with people, and many other things.

I've recently left my position as a frontend engineer at Reactwise, and this year my focus is on growing my contracting and consultancy firm 508.dev, as well as finding a way to generate revenue outside of selling my labor. I also hope to make many FOSS contributions this year.

Family

I'm engaged to be married, in October. My parents left Taiwan in November and are planning on returning as soon as they can. I have several fish tanks, mostly filled with shrimp.

Career

I've just finished working as a software engineer at the YC startup Reactwise. We had to part ways after a grant required all employees to be UK resident, and I didn't want to move there. For the next month, I'm taking a break, focusing on Mandarin and finally picking up some of the programming languages I've wanted to for years. I'm growing a software engineering contracting and consultancy firm called 508.dev. We're always open to new members joining, and also have plenty of time available across our engineers for more contracting work. I'm also hosting coworking events and hackathons, as well as trying out some content creation, with subjects such as ambient coworking videos, and life in Taiwan.
