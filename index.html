A bit about me

I'm Caleb. I have a B.A. in Creative Writing and work as a Software Engineer in San Francisco.

My interests include writing and programming. I intend to make a career out of the latter and dream to make a mildly lucrative hobby out of the former. If you are interested in seeing some of my work, check out my github or navigate to the "portfolio" section of this website.

I live for travel. I am lucky enough to have experienced a variety of cultures in my travels, and am proud that my efforts have landed me an intermediate Chinese language ability and a moderate Japanese language ability. I have written about many of my trips at ablate.blogspot.com.

In the interest of self-improvement, I welcome all feedback to my work. I am always interested in opportunities to collaborate, be it in writing, filming, or programming. If you have any questions, comments, or lucrative business proposals, please feel free to leave a comment on one of my blogs, message me on linkedin, or send me an email at rogersjcaleb@gmail.com.
